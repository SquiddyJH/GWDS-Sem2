Our Services

We offer a range of services here at JCloud which are based on the complexity of the request. If it's and installation we charge based on the amount of devices and the man power required to install them.

When it comes to repairs we won't charge you the same prices of the manufacturer over small issues such as a cracked screen. We promise to charge a fair price for everything we can do.

Upgrades people, upgrades. You will get money off when you trade in your old devices for a new one. We carry as many models as we can to make sure you can find the one that's right for you.
